# Account Access and Membership

The site depends on your WordPress sign-in state. If you are signed in, the app can usually get the account access it needs. If you are not signed in, some parts of the site may still load, but account-based actions may not work.

If the site tells you that account setup is required, it usually means one of two things. Either you are not signed in, or the app does not currently have the account access it expects for your session.

The simplest fix is to sign in again and reload the app page.

Membership and account access matter because they affect what the site can do on your behalf. A page might open, but a run might still fail later if your account is not fully ready.

If you can open the site but cannot save provider keys or start runs, check your sign-in and account state before anything else.

If that still does not fix the problem, read [common-problems.md](common-problems.md).
